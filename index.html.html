About Me

Passion, Experience & Dedication in Every Design

Samrin Shaikh

Professional Mehndi Artist

Creating Art That Touches Hearts

Hello! I am Samrin Shaikh, a passionate mehndi artist dedicated to making every occasion special with beautiful henna designs. With 10 years of experience in this field, I have had the privilege of adorning countless brides and clients with intricate and stunning mehndi patterns.

I provide all types of mehndi patterns — from traditional bridal designs to modern Arabic styles, from simple elegant patterns to heavy full-hand designs. Every design is crafted with love and attention to detail.

I offer home services all over India, so you can get your mehndi done in the comfort of your own home. For areas near Ujjain and Dewas, travel charges are included. For other cities, travel charges are applied separately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

How many days before should I book?
You should book at least 2 days before your event date. You can book anytime before that, but we do not accept bookings 1 day before the event.
Do you provide home service?
Yes! Home service is available all over India. For areas near Ujjain and Dewas, travel charges are included. For other cities, travel charges are applied separately.
What types of mehndi do you offer?
We offer all types of mehndi patterns including Bridal, Arabic, Indo-Arabic, Rajasthani, Simple & Elegant, and Feet Mehndi designs.
How much time does it take?
The duration depends on the design complexity. Simple designs take 30-60 minutes, while heavy bridal mehndi can take 3-5 hours. We will inform you the estimated time when you book.
What are your prices?
Prices vary depending on the design type and complexity. Please contact us on WhatsApp at 7771962386 for detailed package information and pricing.
Do I need to come to a studio?
No, you don't need to come to a studio. We provide home service. However, if you prefer to visit, that can also be arranged. Home service is our primary offering.
